My Top 5 Movies Of All Time

By Uddhav

Movies are something many of us hold dear to all of us, so here I will be listing my Top 5 Movies of all time

1:- Conjuring

This Movie is a classic of Horror Movies. I still remember the scene on the cabinet. It was one of the best jumpscare scenes ever.

2:- Weathering With You

A box office hit. With stunning visuals and even better story. Honestly, This made my heart flutter a bit.

3:- Your Name

This one right here. It's a mix of every emotion you can emotionally feel. It has just the right amount of everything.

4:- The Dark Knight

I just f-ing loved the way Heath Ledger fully showcased the 'Insanity' that Joker possesses. Also Christian Bale potrayed the character of Batman really well. As a big fan of comic and fiction, this movie was really a sight to see.

5:- Inception

This right here is a movie that actually makes you question reality. And I just love it because it caused a scene in my coaching one time.
